Sec. 01 The part everyone else gets wrong

Your data. Your hardware.
Full stop.

Every "modern" field-service platform wants your job history, client list and pricing living in their cloud, behind their subscription, on their terms. White Van Ops takes the opposite bet: the entire system — application, web server and PostgreSQL database — installs on a computer you own.

  • The database ships in the installerIndustrial-grade PostgreSQL is bundled and configured automatically on first launch. No IT project, no separate download, no DBA.
  • No cloud dependencyInternet down? Vendor "sunsets" a product? Price hike email? None of it can touch a system running in your own office.
  • Leave anytime, take everythingYour data sits in a standard PostgreSQL database with CSV exports built in. No export fees, no ransom, no proprietary lock-in.
  • Private by architectureEvery install gets its own unique encryption secret and database credentials, generated on your machine. Field access runs directly through your own office router — no middlemen, no subscriptions, and not our servers.

Sec. 02 Everything a service fleet runs on

Eight modules. One dashboard.

Built for HVAC, electrical, plumbing and specialty trade contractors running 2–25 vans.

Scheduling & Jobs

Job ledger with clients, line items, crew assignment and recurring job templates that generate themselves.

Fleet & Service

Track every van, its maintenance history and repair reports. Know which vehicle is due before it breaks down.

Inventory Control

Warehouse and per-van stock levels with minimum thresholds. Completing a job deducts parts from the right van automatically.

Time Tracking

Techs log hours against jobs from their phone. Admins see labor cost per job in real time.

QuickBooks-Ready Export

One-click CSV exports for invoices and time, with a sync-lock so nothing gets double-billed.

Roles & Audit Log

Superuser, admin and tech roles with forced password changes and a full audit trail of every change.

Offline-First Field App

Techs lose signal? No problem. The PWA caches jobs locally and silently syncs when reconnected.

Ironclad Local Backups

Configure a target mirror directory. The system automatically executes a full pg_dump backup every night.
